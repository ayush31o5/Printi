Finding WiFi Direct Details on Your Printer

To set up WiFi Direct on your printer, you will need to locate the following details:

  • SSID: The wireless network name. You can usually find this printed on a label on your printer, in the printer's user manual, or in the network settings menu.
  • Password (Passphrase): This is often provided on the same label as the SSID or in the documentation. If your printer requires a password, it may be preset or configurable via the printer’s settings.
  • Security Type: Most modern printers use WPA2 (or WPA/WPA2) for secure WiFi Direct connections. Check your printer’s network settings or manual to confirm this.
  • Encryption/Authentication: In some cases, you might need to verify additional details like the encryption method (e.g., CCMP) or authentication type (commonly, open or WPA2-PSK).

If you are uncertain about any of these details, please consult your printer's user manual or visit the support page on the manufacturer's website.
